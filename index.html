What is Alumni Connect?

A digital bridge between current MITAOE students and alumni working in leading universities and companies around the world.

For Students

Get real-world guidance on internships, placements, higher studies, and projects directly from people who have been in your shoes.

For Alumni

Give back to your college, share your journey, and discover talented students for referrals, collaborations, and hiring.

For MITAOE

Build a stronger, connected community that supports continuous learning, innovation, and career growth.
